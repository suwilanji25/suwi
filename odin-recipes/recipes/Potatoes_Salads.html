Potatoes Salads

Discription

potatoes salads with eggs,gherkins and creamy salad dressing

    Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons pickled gherkins, chopped
  • 1/4 cup creamy salad dressing
  • 4 hard boiled eggs, cut into wedges, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, or as desired

      • Steps

    1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender about 20 minutes;drain and rinse with cold water to cool.Remove and discard potato skins.Slice potatoes.
    2. Whisk vinger,sugar,salt and pepper together in a large bowl;add potatoes,onion and chopped gherkin.Stir salad dressing garnish with the remaining eggs wedges and parsley.cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
